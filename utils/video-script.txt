# BrainMark - AI-Powered Tab Management
## Professional Explainer Video Script for Narakeet

---

## Scene 1: Hook & Problem Statement
### Duration: 15 seconds

We've all been there.

Twenty browser tabs open. Thirty. Fifty.

You're researching a project, shopping for a gift, and planning a vacation all at once.

But which tab was that important article in?

Where did you see that price comparison?

The tab chaos is real, and it's costing you time.

---

## Scene 2: Introduce Solution
### Duration: 12 seconds

Meet BrainMark.

The Chrome extension that uses AI to transform your tab chaos into organized intelligence.

Built with Chrome's powerful Built-in AI APIs, BrainMark understands what you're working on and helps you stay focused.

---

## Scene 3: Feature 1 - Intelligent Tab Organization
### Duration: 20 seconds

Let's see how it works.

Open BrainMark from your Chrome toolbar.

Click "AI Cluster" and watch the magic happen.

BrainMark analyzes all your open tabs using advanced AI and automatically groups them by purpose.

Job search tabs here. Shopping tabs there. Research articles over here.

Each group becomes a project you can save, organize, and return to anytime.

No more hunting through endless tabs.

---

## Scene 4: Feature 2 - Deep Research Capabilities
### Duration: 25 seconds

But BrainMark goes beyond organization.

Navigate to any webpage and click the "Research" button.

Within seconds, BrainMark's AI analyzes the entire page and generates a comprehensive research report.

You get an eighty to one-twenty word summary with specific data points.

Four detailed key findings with evidence-based analysis.

Expert-level frequently asked questions with comprehensive answers.

Even structured data like tables and lists, all extracted automatically.

It's like having a research assistant built right into your browser.

---

## Scene 5: Feature 3 - Dig Deeper
### Duration: 18 seconds

Want to know more?

Use the "Dig Deeper" feature at the bottom of any research page.

Type your question: "What are the main benefits mentioned?"

BrainMark's AI reads the page context and provides detailed, intelligent answers.

Ask as many questions as you want.

Copy answers. Remove them when done.

It's interactive research powered by AI.

## Scene 6: Privacy & Technology
### Duration: 20 seconds

Here's what makes BrainMark special.

It uses Chrome's Built-in AI APIs for one hundred percent client-side processing.

That means your data never leaves your device.

No cloud uploads. No tracking. Complete privacy.

The AI runs directly in your browser, making it fast, secure, and offline-capable.

For extended compatibility, there's an optional Gemini API fallback, but Chrome's AI is always prioritized.
